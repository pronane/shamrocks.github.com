North London Shamrocks Youth welcomes children of all abilities (absolute beginners welcome!) in the following age groups: U8 Born after 1 Jan 2004
U10 Born after 1 Jan 2002
U12 Born after 1 Jan 2000
U14 Born after 1 Jan 1998
U16 Born after 1 Jan 1996

Training

Football training is every Saturday from 1.30-3pm at Old Ignatians RFC, Donkey Lane (off Carterhatch Lane), Enfield EN1 3PL.
The competitive Gaelic football season starts as the soccer and rugby seasons come to an end so compliments any child’s sporting schedule. For further information contact the Youth Secretary tonyamcaloon@hotmail.com/07971 535 130 or Youth PRO bernie.harrington@sky.com /07903 113 907
NLSY is actively involved in promoting and coaching Gaelic football in a number of north London schools including St Ignatius College, Finchley Catholic High School, Our Lady of Muswell R.C.P.S and St George’s R.C.P.S
