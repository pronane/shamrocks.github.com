Come and try GAA day in Enfield

The Enfield "Come and Try" Day is Enfields Annual Sports Event for young people who would like to try out different Sports. It takes place in Donkey Lane on the 9th of June between 2 and 4pm.

North London Shamrocks Youth will be there and as part of the day we will have a "Adults v Children" game similar to what we had last year. The adults are gunning for revenge after their shock defeat last year and a Donegal style training regime has been in place to get them up to shape. It promises to be a fun day for all.

Pat Spillane meets the Shamrocks Youth

On the 2nd of March we had Pat Spillane down do give some training to the youth, a great day was had by all as Pat gave us some great tips, including his famous thoughts on the fist pass!!!

Blitz Sunday 21st April

Please note there is a Blitz taking place at Broomfield Park this weekend the 21st of April - ALL WELCOME!! This is just a friendly competition as kids of Palmers Green and Enfield get to know each other and for paretns to meet and give out about the trainers!!. Hope to see you there.
