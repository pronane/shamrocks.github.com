Training

Training

Posted by Paul Ronane on January 07, 2012  •  Comments (64)  •  Full article

Training for the up-coming season has started, taking place every Tuesday and Thursday at Enfield Ignatians RFC, Donkey Lane, EN1 3PL, Enfield.

Training starts on the pitch at 7.45pm and lasts for an hour. The club welcomes all new players of any ability who wish to jopin the club. For transport to training or for further details please contact the club by email at info@theshamrocks.co.uk or contact management directly:
Tom 'Tosh' Kilcommons - 07931584799
Vinny Murphy - 07956499439
Kenny Fagan - 07944294795

The club is fortunate to have excellent standards of facilities at their disposal thanks to EIRFC, which includes a full lenght pitch, 4 changing rooms, flood light training pitch and club house bar.
