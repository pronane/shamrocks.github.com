Contact Us

Posted by Paul Ronane on January 17, 2012 3:50 a.m.  •  Comments (64)  •  Full article

North London Shamrocks Gaelic Football Club are always keen to recruit new players or social members. If you are new to London the club can help make the transition easier for you by helping to secure accommodation or employment in your chosen field.
We are based in the Enfield area of North London but with players from all over London there is no problem with getting transport to our training ground. Our training facilities are the envy of most London clubs as we have access to our own home pitch and club house.

If you are interested in playing Gaelic football and expanding your social network please contact us by email at info@theshamrocks.co.uk or contact the management team directly.
