Welcome to North London Shamrocks

Posted by Paul Ronane on January 07, 2012  •  Comments (64)  •  Full article

The North London Shamrocks are based in the Palmer's Green/Enfield area of North London. Players of the club hail from all over North London. Our training faclilites are the envy of most London clubs. We are also one of very few London clubs who have access to their own home pitch.

We're also a very friendly bunch of lads and always welcome new faces down to the club. If you're interested in playing Gaelic football and expanding your social network please get in touch. New arrivals to London have also been helped find accommodation and work in the past. We are currently recruiting for the upcoming 2013 season. If you would like to train and play with one of London's most progressive Gaelic Football clubs please contact us.
Bunaíodh seamróga Londain thuaidh i 2005 faoi cónaisc Naomh Threasa as Ponders end agus Shannon Rovers/Sam Maguire ó Wood Green/Tottenham. Tá muid suite i dtuaisceart Londain i Palmers Green/Winchmore Hill/Enfield. Tá roinnt maith imreoirí againn ó Eireann agus Londain go maith le triúr imreoir as Ghaoth Dobhair i nDún na nGall agus imreoir amhain as Naomh Padraig an Fhairce in nGaillimh. Siocar gur club réasunta úr ata ionainn is i an aidhm atá againn no baill ura a tharraingh isteach. Is grúpa cairdiúil muid a chuireann failte mhór roimh baill úra.
